# Destination Authority Ownership Framework

The objective is not ranking for keywords. The objective is becoming the default travel authority associated with Spiti, Kashmir, and Ladakh. We aim to own the entire search journey.

## 1. Destination Ownership Score

For each core destination (Spiti, Kashmir, Ladakh, Uttarakhand, Thailand, Dubai), a composite score (out of 100) will be tracked based on the following three pillars:

### Pillar 1: Authority Metrics
*   Total Indexed Pages for Destination
*   Search Impressions & Clicks
*   Rankings (Top 3, Top 10)
*   Backlinks & Internal Link Density

### Pillar 2: Commercial Metrics
*   Leads Generated
*   Bookings Secured
*   Total Revenue Driven

### Pillar 3: AI Metrics
*   AI Overview (SGE) Visibility
*   ChatGPT Citation Presence
*   Gemini Citation Presence
*   Perplexity Citation Presence

## 2. Ownership Targets

To measure systemic growth, we will track these scores against rigid timelines:

### 12-Month Targets
*   **Spiti:** 70/100
*   **Kashmir:** 60/100
*   **Ladakh:** 55/100

### 24-Month Targets
*   **Spiti:** 90/100
*   **Kashmir:** 80/100
*   **Ladakh:** 80/100

## 3. Quarterly Review
Every 90 days, the SEO lead must compile and report:
*   Current Ownership Score per Destination
*   Competitor Benchmark (Thrillophilia, WanderOn, MakeMyTrip)
*   Growth Since Last Quarter
*   Next Priority Actions to close the gap
